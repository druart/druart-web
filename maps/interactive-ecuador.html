Geography of Ecuador.

Some Information

Officially the Republic of Ecuador (Spanish: República del Ecuador), is located in northwestern South America, bordered by Colombia on the north, Peru on the east and south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. Ecuador also includes the Galápagos Islands in the Pacific, about 1000 km west of the mainland. The capital city is Quito. Official language Spanish.
